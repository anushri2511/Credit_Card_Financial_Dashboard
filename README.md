# 💳 Credit Card Weekly Dashboard – Power BI Project

## 📌 Project Objective

To develop a **comprehensive weekly credit card dashboard** that provides real-time insights into key performance metrics and trends.
This enables stakeholders to **monitor operations, track revenue and identify customer behavior patterns effectively**.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 💰 Revenue & Transactions

* Total Revenue: **57M**
* Total Transaction Amount: **46M**
* Total Interest Earned: **8M**
* Revenue Growth: **+0.29% (weekly)**

### 👥 Customer Insights

* Male Customers Revenue: **31M**
* Female Customers Revenue: **26M**
  👉 Males contribute slightly more to overall revenue

### 💳 Card Category Performance

* **Blue & Silver cards** contribute to **93% of total transactions**

### 🌍 Regional Insights

* **Top States:** TX, NY, CA
* Contribution: **68% of total revenue**

### ⚡ Operational Metrics

* Activation Rate: **57.46%**
* Delinquency Rate: **6.06%**
